💰 Benefits of Daily SIP Investment

Why investing every day beats monthly SIP — backed by math and discipline

📈
Extra Compounding Power
Daily SIP compounds 365 times/year vs 12 times for monthly SIP. Over 20 years, this gives 3-8% extra returns on the same investment amount.
☕
Chai Money = Wealth
Just ₹100-500/day (cost of chai + snack) can build ₹1 Cr+ corpus over 20 years at 12% return. Small daily habit, massive long-term result.
💪
Rupee Cost Averaging (Daily)
Daily SIP buys units at 365 different NAVs per year vs 12. This smooths out market volatility far better than monthly investing.
🚀
Recover Full EMI Cost
A parallel daily SIP alongside your EMI can recover your entire loan cost (principal + interest) by the time your loan ends. Free house/car!
💰
Beat Inflation Automatically
At 12% SIP return vs 6% inflation, your real wealth grows at 6% p.a. Daily investing ensures you never miss a compounding day.
🌱
Build Discipline Like Brushing
Daily SIP becomes a habit like brushing teeth. No mental burden of big monthly payments. ₹200/day feels easier than ₹6,000/month psychologically.
📊
Lower Market Timing Risk
With 365 entry points per year, you eliminate the risk of investing all your money on a single bad day each month.
🌟
Start with Just ₹100/day
Many AMCs now allow daily SIP starting ₹100. No minimum lock-in, fully liquid, 100% online, SEBI regulated. Perfect for beginners.
💸
Tax Benefits (ELSS Daily SIP)
Daily SIP in ELSS funds gives Section 80C tax deduction up to ₹1.5L/year. Each day's investment has its own 3-year lock-in, providing staggered liquidity.
