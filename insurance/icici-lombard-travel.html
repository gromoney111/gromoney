Frequently Asked Questions — ICICI Lombard Travel

Is ICICI Lombard travel insurance valid for Schengen visa?

Yes, all ICICI Lombard international travel plans are Schengen compliant with €30,000+ medical cover, repatriation, and emergency evacuation. The digital policy certificate is accepted by all Schengen country embassies for visa applications.

How much does ICICI Lombard travel insurance cost for USA?

A 15-day USA trip with $250K medical cover costs approximately ₹3,500-6,000 depending on age and plan variant. $500K cover costs ₹5,000-9,000. Student annual plans for US range from ₹12,000-30,000 based on coverage level.

Does ICICI Lombard cover pre-existing conditions during travel?

Standard plans exclude pre-existing conditions. Premium/Platinum variants cover acute episodes of pre-existing conditions (sudden emergencies requiring immediate treatment). Always declare conditions truthfully during purchase for valid claims.

What is covered if my flight is cancelled?

Trip cancellation cover reimburses prepaid, non-refundable expenses (flights, hotels, tours) if cancelled due to covered reasons — medical emergency, death of family member, natural disaster at destination, or visa refusal. Coverage: up to $10,000 in premium plans.

Can I buy ICICI Lombard travel insurance at the airport?

Yes, you can buy online via phone at the airport as policies are issued instantly. However, we recommend purchasing at least 24-48 hours before departure for trip cancellation benefits to be active and visa compliance documentation readiness.
