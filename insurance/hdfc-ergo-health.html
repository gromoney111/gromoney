HDFC Ergo Health Insurance — Optima Secure, Suraksha & More

Part of the HDFC Group and ERGO International AG (Munich Re), offering tech-driven health insurance with 13,000+ network hospitals since 2002.

About HDFC Ergo General Insurance

HDFC Ergo General Insurance Company is a joint venture between HDFC Ltd (now merged with HDFC Bank) and ERGO International AG — part of Munich Re Group, one of the world's largest reinsurers. Established in 2002, HDFC Ergo is India's 3rd largest private general insurer with a strong focus on health insurance innovation.

The company leverages HDFC Bank's massive 70-million customer base for distribution while applying Munich Re's global risk expertise to product design. With gross written premium exceeding ₹15,000 crore and claim settlement ratio above 96%, HDFC Ergo is synonymous with reliability in health coverage.

Popular HDFC Ergo Health Plans

Optima Secure

HDFC Ergo's flagship health plan with coverage up to ₹75 Lakh and unique "Multiplier" benefit that increases sum insured by 50% annually for no-claim years (up to 3x). Features unlimited automatic restoration and zero room rent limits.

  • Sum insured: ₹3L to ₹75 Lakh
  • Multiplier: +50% per year (up to 3x SI)
  • Unlimited automatic restoration
  • No room rent or ICU sub-limits

My Health Suraksha

A customizable health plan where you pick and choose coverage modules — base hospitalization, critical illness, personal accident, daily hospital cash, and OPD. Pay only for what you need, making it highly affordable and flexible.

  • Modular design — choose coverage blocks
  • Base cover: ₹3L to ₹50 Lakh
  • Optional OPD, critical illness, maternity
  • Affordable — pay for what you select only

Health Plus

An upgrade plan designed to complement employer-provided group health insurance. Covers exclusions, enhances sum insured, and bridges gaps in corporate policies without duplicating base coverage.

  • Top-up + super top-up options
  • Covers gaps in employer insurance
  • Deductible linked to corporate cover
  • Family floater available

Optima Restore

Features the unique "Restore" benefit — if your sum insured is exhausted during the year, it gets automatically restored to the full amount for subsequent unrelated illnesses. No extra premium for this restoration feature.

  • Restore benefit — full SI refill for new illness
  • Cumulative bonus: 50% per year (up to 100%)
  • Floater option for family
  • Pre & post hospitalization: 60 + 180 days

HDFC Ergo Claim Process

1

Cashless Request

Show e-health card at network hospital. Hospital sends pre-auth via HDFC Ergo portal. Planned admission: 48 hours prior; Emergency: within 24 hours of admission.

2

Quick Approval

HDFC Ergo processes pre-auth within 30 minutes. Approved amount communicated to hospital. Enhancement requests handled within 2 hours during treatment.

3

Cashless Treatment

Receive treatment without paying upfront. Hospital settles directly with HDFC Ergo. Patient pays only non-covered items and co-pay (if any) at discharge.

4

Reimbursement

For non-network hospitals: submit bills within 15 days of discharge via app or courier. HDFC Ergo settles within 7-14 working days post document verification.

Frequently Asked Questions — HDFC Ergo Health

What is the Multiplier benefit in Optima Secure?

The Multiplier increases your base sum insured by 50% for each claim-free year, up to a maximum of 3x the original SI. For example, a ₹10 Lakh plan grows to ₹15L, ₹20L, ₹25L, and finally ₹30L over 4 claim-free years — all without any premium increase.

How is My Health Suraksha different from Optima Secure?

My Health Suraksha is modular — you choose coverage blocks (hospitalization, critical illness, OPD, daily cash) and pay only for selected modules. Optima Secure is a comprehensive all-inclusive plan. Suraksha is more affordable but requires careful module selection.

Does HDFC Ergo cover pre-existing diseases?

Yes, all HDFC Ergo health plans cover pre-existing diseases after a waiting period of 48 months (standard) or 36 months (premium variants). During the waiting period, hospitalizations for those specific conditions are excluded.

Can I buy HDFC Ergo health insurance through HDFC Bank?

Yes, HDFC Ergo policies are available through all HDFC Bank branches, internet banking, and the HDFC Bank app. You can also pay premiums in EMIs on HDFC Bank credit cards at zero additional cost.

What is the entry age for HDFC Ergo health plans?

Optima Secure accepts ages 18-65 (adult) and 91 days to 25 years (dependent child). My Health Suraksha accepts 18-65. Renewal is guaranteed for life regardless of claims or health changes.
