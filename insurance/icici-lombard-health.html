About ICICI Lombard General Insurance

ICICI Lombard General Insurance Company is a joint venture between ICICI Bank and Fairfax Financial Holdings (Canada). Established in 2001, it is India's largest private sector general insurance company by gross written premium. The company offers a comprehensive suite of products across health, motor, travel, home, and commercial insurance segments.

With a GWP exceeding ₹23,000 crore, ICICI Lombard is a technology leader in India's insurance industry — pioneering AI-based claims, telemedicine integration, and instant policy issuance. The company serves 3.6+ crore customers through 290+ offices, ICICI Bank branches, and digital channels.

ICICI Lombard Claim Process

1

Register

Intimate claim via IL Take Care app, website, WhatsApp, or call 1800-266-9725. For cashless: present ID at network hospital and request pre-authorization.

2

Pre-Auth

Hospital sends pre-auth digitally. ICICI Lombard's AI system processes within 30 minutes for planned admissions. Emergency cases: 60 minutes maximum.

3

Treatment

Receive treatment cashlessly. Hospital bills ICICI Lombard directly. Patient pays only non-covered consumables and co-pay (if any) at discharge.

4

Settlement

Cashless settled directly. Reimbursement: submit bills within 15 days via app (photo upload). AI-verified claims paid within 5-7 working days via NEFT.

Frequently Asked Questions — ICICI Lombard Health

What is the best ICICI Lombard health plan for families?

Complete Health Insurance (₹10-25L floater) is ideal for families — it covers all members, includes unlimited restoration, modern treatments, and booster benefit. For budget plans, Health Booster super top-up at ₹5,000/year adds ₹10-20L over existing cover.

Does ICICI Lombard cover robotic surgery?

Yes, ICICI Lombard covers 17 modern treatments including robotic surgery, stem cell therapy, gene therapy, immunotherapy, oral chemotherapy, and balloon sinuplasty — all at no additional premium in Complete Health and Elevate plans.

How does the Health Booster super top-up work?

Health Booster activates after your base health cover (corporate/individual) is exhausted. Choose a deductible matching your base cover, and Health Booster covers expenses above that limit up to ₹5-50 Lakh. Premiums are very affordable (₹2,000-8,000/year).

Is there a co-pay in ICICI Lombard plans?

Complete Health Insurance has zero co-pay for ages below 60. For 60+ members, a 10-20% co-pay may apply depending on variant. Health Booster has no co-pay. Always check plan-specific terms before purchase.

Can I use teleconsultation with ICICI Lombard?

Yes, IL Take Care plans offer unlimited free teleconsultations with specialists via partner apps. You can consult doctors online for minor ailments, get e-prescriptions, and access digital health records — all at no extra cost.
