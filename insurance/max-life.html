About Max Life Insurance

Max Life Insurance Company Limited is a joint venture between Max Financial Services and Axis Bank. Established in 2000, Max Life has earned a reputation as one of India's most trusted life insurance companies, consistently winning awards for claims settlement, customer service, and product innovation.

With assets under management exceeding ₹1.4 lakh crore and over 1.5 crore customers served, Max Life operates through 270+ offices across India. The company partners with Axis Bank for bancassurance, reaching millions of customers through the bank's vast network of 4,700+ branches.

Frequently Asked Questions — Max Life

Is Max Life a reliable insurance company?

Yes, Max Life is highly reliable with a 99.51% claim settlement ratio, Axis Bank backing, solvency ratio of 191%, and 24+ years of operational excellence. It's consistently rated among India's top 5 private life insurers.

What is the premium for Max Life Smart Secure Plus ₹1 Crore?

For a 30-year-old non-smoking male, Smart Secure Plus ₹1 Crore cover for 30 years costs approximately ₹650-850/month. Premiums vary based on age, lifestyle, sum assured, and riders. Women receive 5-8% discount.

Can I buy Max Life plans through Axis Bank?

Yes, Max Life plans are available at all 4,700+ Axis Bank branches through bancassurance. You can also purchase online through the Max Life website or through certified partners like GroMoney Capital.

How does Max Life Online Savings Plan work?

You pay premiums for a limited period (5/7/10 years) and receive guaranteed additions each year. At maturity, you get sum assured + accumulated guaranteed additions as a lump sum or regular income. IRR ranges from 6.2-7% based on tenure.

What critical illnesses does Max Life cover?

Max Life's Critical Illness rider covers 64 critical conditions including cancer, heart attack, stroke, kidney failure, major organ transplant, paralysis, and more. Payout is a lump sum on diagnosis, independent of actual treatment costs.
