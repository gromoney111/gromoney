HDFC Life Insurance — Plans, Benefits & Claim Process

One of India's leading private life insurers with 99%+ claim settlement ratio, offering comprehensive term, savings, and investment plans since 2000.

About HDFC Life Insurance

HDFC Life Insurance Company Limited is a joint venture between HDFC Ltd (now merged with HDFC Bank) and abrdn plc (formerly Standard Life Aberdeen). Established in 2000, HDFC Life has emerged as one of India's top private life insurers with a diverse portfolio covering protection, savings, investment, retirement, and health solutions.

The company serves over 6.7 crore lives through individual and group policies and has assets under management exceeding ₹2.7 lakh crore. HDFC Life is listed on BSE and NSE, and is known for its customer-centric innovation and digital-first approach to insurance distribution.

Popular HDFC Life Plans

Click2Protect Life

HDFC Life's flagship online term insurance plan offering comprehensive life cover with multiple plan options — Life Protect, Extra Protect, and Income Protect. Covers up to ₹10 Crore with affordable premiums starting from ₹490/month.

  • Cover options: Lump sum, monthly income, or both
  • Optional riders: Critical illness, accidental death
  • Special premium discount for women
  • Whole life cover option up to age 99

Sanchay Plus

A non-linked, non-participating savings insurance plan offering guaranteed returns and life cover. Choose from multiple benefit options — guaranteed maturity, guaranteed income, or life long income for systematic wealth creation.

  • Guaranteed returns — no market risk
  • Long-term income option (up to 30 years)
  • Return of premium on death
  • Flexible premium payment terms (5-12 years)

ProGrowth Plus

A ULIP (Unit Linked Insurance Plan) combining market-linked growth potential with life insurance coverage. Offers 4 fund options with automatic portfolio management and loyalty additions from the 6th year onwards.

  • Zero premium allocation charge
  • Multiple fund strategy options
  • Loyalty additions from year 6
  • Partial withdrawal after 5-year lock-in

Click2Retire

An online retirement solution that helps build a corpus for your golden years. Combines disciplined savings with market-linked returns and offers a guaranteed lifelong pension starting at your chosen retirement age.

  • Guaranteed vesting benefit
  • Systematic investment through SIP mode
  • Flexible retirement age (45-75 years)
  • Top-up option for additional investment

HDFC Life Claim Process

1

Intimation

Register claim online at hdfclife.com, via the app, or call 1860-267-9999. Provide policy number, date, and cause of claim event.

2

Document Submission

Upload or submit claim form, death/medical certificate, KYC of nominee, policy document, and bank details for settlement.

3

Assessment

HDFC Life's claims team reviews documents within 24-48 hours. For standard cases, no further investigation required — fast-tracked for approval.

4

Payout

Approved claims are settled within 1-3 working days via NEFT. Average claim turnaround: 1.4 days for non-investigated death claims.

Frequently Asked Questions — HDFC Life

What is HDFC Life's claim settlement ratio for 2024-25?

HDFC Life's individual death claim settlement ratio for FY 2024-25 is 99.07% by number of policies, making it one of the top-performing private life insurers in India. This means virtually every valid claim is settled.

Which is the best HDFC Life term plan?

Click2Protect Life is HDFC Life's most popular term plan, offering coverage up to ₹10 Crore with premiums starting from ₹490/month. It includes options for income protection, critical illness cover, and whole-life coverage up to 99 years.

Can I buy HDFC Life insurance without medical tests?

Yes, HDFC Life offers non-medical underwriting for policies up to certain sum assured limits (typically ₹50-75 Lakh for ages below 35). Higher covers require standard medical tests including blood work and ECG.

How is Sanchay Plus different from a fixed deposit?

Sanchay Plus offers guaranteed returns like an FD but with additional life cover, tax benefits under 80C, and tax-free maturity under 10(10D). The effective post-tax returns often exceed FD returns for individuals in higher tax brackets.

What riders can I add to HDFC Life policies?

HDFC Life offers critical illness rider (covering 59 conditions), accidental death benefit, waiver of premium on total permanent disability, hospital care benefit, and additional term rider to enhance base coverage.
