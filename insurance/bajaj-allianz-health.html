Bajaj Allianz General — Health Guard, Ensure & Family First

India's leading private general insurer (Bajaj Finserv + Allianz SE Germany) with 10,000+ cashless hospitals and comprehensive health insurance solutions since 2001.

About Bajaj Allianz General Insurance

Bajaj Allianz General Insurance Company Limited is a joint venture between Bajaj Finserv and Allianz SE, Germany — the world's largest insurance company. Established in 2001, the company is among India's top 3 private general insurers with a gross written premium exceeding ₹18,000 crore.

Known for its AI-driven claims process, extensive digital capabilities, and customer-first innovation, Bajaj Allianz General has consistently won awards for claims excellence, digital innovation, and customer satisfaction. The company operates through 300+ branches and 60,000+ agent touchpoints across India.

Popular Bajaj Allianz Health Plans

Health Guard

Bajaj Allianz's flagship individual and family floater health plan with coverage from ₹1.5 Lakh to ₹50 Lakh. Features automatic restoration of sum insured, 500+ day-care procedures, ambulance cover, and optional critical illness rider.

  • Cover: ₹1.5L to ₹50 Lakh
  • 100% automatic sum insured restoration
  • 500+ day-care procedures
  • Cumulative bonus: 10% per year (max 50%)

Health Ensure

A comprehensive plan with wider coverage including maternity benefit, new-born baby cover, organ donor expenses, and domestic/worldwide emergency cover. Ideal for families planning to expand or those needing broader protection.

  • Maternity cover + new-born baby cover
  • Organ donor hospitalization expenses
  • Worldwide emergency cover option
  • Hospital cash benefit: ₹1,000-5,000/day

Family First Silver

An affordable entry-level family health plan with coverage from ₹2 Lakh to ₹10 Lakh. Designed for middle-income families prioritizing essential hospitalization coverage without premium features at budget-friendly rates.

  • Affordable family floater
  • Cover: ₹2L to ₹10 Lakh
  • Day-care treatment included
  • Pre & post hospitalization: 30 + 60 days

Health Infinity

A premium health plan offering unlimited sum insured (no maximum cap on claims) with features like no co-pay, no room rent limits, second medical opinion, and personalized wellness programs for holistic health management.

  • Unlimited sum insured — no cap
  • No room rent or ICU sub-limits
  • Second medical opinion facility
  • Personalized wellness program

Bajaj Allianz Health Claim Process

1

Cashless Request

Show e-card via Caringly Yours app at network hospital. Hospital sends pre-auth request. Planned: 48 hours prior; Emergency: within 24 hours of admission.

2

AI Approval

Bajaj Allianz AI system processes pre-auth within 20 minutes. Instant approval for standard cases. Enhancement handled within 2 hours during hospitalization.

3

Treatment

Receive treatment without upfront payment. Hospital settles directly with Bajaj Allianz. Patient pays only non-covered items and co-pay (if applicable) at discharge.

4

Reimbursement

For non-network: submit bills within 15 days via app (photo upload). AI-verified documents processed within 7 working days. NEFT transfer to registered bank account.

Frequently Asked Questions — Bajaj Allianz Health

Which Bajaj Allianz health plan is best for family?

Health Guard (₹10-25L floater) is the best all-round family plan with automatic restoration, 500+ day-care procedures, and cumulative bonus. For families planning children, Health Ensure adds maternity and newborn coverage. Budget families can start with Family First Silver.

Does Bajaj Allianz have unlimited health insurance?

Yes, Health Infinity offers unlimited sum insured with no cap on total claims in a year. It also has no room rent limits, no co-pay, and includes second medical opinion and personalized wellness programs. Premium is higher but offers complete financial freedom during medical emergencies.

How fast is Bajaj Allianz cashless approval?

Bajaj Allianz uses AI-powered processing for cashless pre-authorization — standard approvals within 20 minutes. Emergency cases processed within 60 minutes. Real-time tracking available via Caringly Yours app throughout the process.

Does Bajaj Allianz health cover pre-existing diseases?

Yes, all Bajaj Allianz health plans cover pre-existing diseases after a waiting period of 48 months (standard). During this period, hospitalizations for those specific conditions are excluded while other illnesses are fully covered from day 1.

What is the co-pay in Bajaj Allianz Health Guard?

Health Guard has zero co-pay for ages 18-55. For ages 56-65, a 10% co-pay may apply depending on the sum insured variant. Health Infinity has zero co-pay across all ages. Always verify plan-specific terms before buying.
