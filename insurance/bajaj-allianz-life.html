About Bajaj Allianz Life Insurance

Bajaj Allianz Life Insurance Company Limited is a joint venture between Bajaj Finserv Limited — one of India's largest financial conglomerates — and Allianz SE, Germany — the world's largest insurance group. Established in 2001, the company combines Bajaj's deep Indian market expertise with Allianz's global insurance knowledge and best practices.

Bajaj Allianz Life has served over 6 crore customers and manages assets exceeding ₹1 lakh crore. Known for its technology-first approach, the company was among the first to introduce AI-based instant claims settlement, WhatsApp-based servicing, and completely paperless onboarding processes.

Popular Bajaj Allianz Life Plans

Bajaj Allianz eTouch

An affordable online term insurance plan offering pure life cover up to ₹1 Crore with one of the lowest premiums in the industry. Simple, no-frills protection ideal for young families seeking maximum cover at minimum cost.

  • Cover up to ₹1 Crore — online only
  • Premiums from ₹375/month (25-yr male)
  • Policy term: 10 to 30 years
  • Optional accidental death rider

Smart Protect Goal

A comprehensive term plan with multiple payout options — life protect, income protect, and income plus protect. Offers return of premium variant ensuring 100% premium refund on survival, combining protection with savings.

  • Multiple sum assured options up to ₹5 Cr
  • Return of Premium (ROP) variant
  • Critical illness cover (40 conditions)
  • Joint life option for couples

Assured Wealth Goal

A non-linked guaranteed returns plan providing life cover with wealth accumulation through guaranteed additions. Limited premium payment with lump sum maturity benefit for long-term financial goals.

  • Guaranteed additions every year
  • Limited pay: 5, 8, or 10 years
  • Maturity: Sum assured + guaranteed additions
  • IRR of 6.2-6.8% based on term

Goal Based Saving

A ULIP plan designed for wealth creation with multiple fund options and zero premium allocation charges. Features life stage-based automatic fund rebalancing to optimize returns based on your risk profile and time horizon.

  • Zero allocation charges
  • 10 fund options across asset classes
  • Automatic life stage rebalancing
  • Loyalty additions from year 5

Frequently Asked Questions — Bajaj Allianz Life

Why is Bajaj Allianz Life's solvency ratio so high?

Bajaj Allianz Life maintains a 414% solvency ratio (vs 150% IRDAI requirement) due to conservative reserve management, Allianz's global risk practices, and strong profitability. This means extreme financial safety for policyholders.

Is Bajaj Allianz eTouch good for term insurance?

Yes, eTouch is one of India's most affordable online term plans with premiums starting ₹375/month for 25-year-old males. It's ideal for pure protection needs up to ₹1 Crore without bells and whistles.

What is Return of Premium in Smart Protect Goal?

The ROP variant returns 100% of premiums paid (excluding rider and GST) if you survive the full policy term. While premiums are 40-60% higher than standard term, you get complete money back plus life cover.

How quickly does Bajaj Allianz Life settle claims?

Standard claims are typically settled within 1-2 business days. AI-eligible claims (policies 3+ years, natural death, documentation complete) can be settled same-day. Overall claim ratio: 98.34%.

Can I buy Bajaj Allianz Life plans without medical tests?

Yes, for ages below 40 and sum assured up to ₹75 Lakh, many plans are issued without medical tests using AI-based underwriting. Higher covers or older ages require standard medical examinations.
