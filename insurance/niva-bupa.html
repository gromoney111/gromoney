Niva Bupa Health Insurance — Plans, Benefits & Cashless Network

Formerly Max Bupa, Niva Bupa is a joint venture with UK's Bupa Group offering comprehensive health plans with global healthcare expertise since 2008.

About Niva Bupa Health Insurance

Niva Bupa Health Insurance (formerly Max Bupa) is a joint venture between Fettle Tone LLP (True North Fund) and Bupa Singapore Holdings Pte. Ltd — part of the UK-based Bupa Group serving 43 million customers in 190 countries. Established in 2008, Niva Bupa is among India's fastest-growing standalone health insurers.

The company offers innovative health insurance products with a focus on customer experience, wellness integration, and hassle-free claims. With 10,000+ network hospitals, Niva Bupa has earned a reputation for transparent policies with minimal exclusions and no hidden sub-limits in premium plans.

Niva Bupa Claim Process

1

Intimate

Inform Niva Bupa via app, website, email claims@nivabupa.com, or call 1800-200-4488 (toll-free). For planned admission, intimate 48 hours prior.

2

Pre-Authorization

Hospital sends cashless request. Niva Bupa approves within 30 minutes for network hospitals. Dedicated claim manager assigned for guidance throughout.

3

Treatment

Undergo treatment cashlessly. Final bill sent to Niva Bupa for settlement. Only non-covered items or co-pay (if applicable) paid by patient.

4

Settlement

Cashless claims settled directly with hospital. Reimbursement claims processed within 7 working days of document submission via NEFT to your account.

Frequently Asked Questions — Niva Bupa

Is Niva Bupa the same as Max Bupa?

Yes, Max Bupa was rebranded to Niva Bupa in 2021 following a change in ownership from Max India to Fettle Tone LLP (True North Fund). Bupa Group continues as the international JV partner. All existing policies continue seamlessly under the new name.

Which Niva Bupa plan is best for a young couple?

Health Companion (₹10-15L floater) is ideal for young couples — it covers maternity (after 9-month wait), mental health, annual check-ups, and has a cumulative bonus of 10% per year. For premium coverage, ReAssure 2.0 offers unlimited restoration and worldwide cover.

Does Niva Bupa cover pre-existing diseases?

Yes, pre-existing diseases are covered after a waiting period of 36-48 months in standard plans. Senior First Platinum reduces this to just 12 months. Conditions like diabetes, hypertension, and thyroid are fully covered post-waiting period.

What is the co-pay in Niva Bupa plans?

Most Niva Bupa plans for ages 18-60 have zero co-pay. Senior plans (61+) may have 10-20% co-pay depending on the variant chosen. ReAssure 2.0 Platinum has zero co-pay across all age groups.

Can I get Niva Bupa coverage for parents above 60?

Yes, Senior First plan accepts entries from 46-75 years. It offers coverage from ₹5-20 Lakh with features like reduced PED waiting, wellness coaching, and no medical test requirement for Platinum variant. Lifetime renewability guaranteed.
