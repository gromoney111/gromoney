Allianz's global network of assistance companies — Allianz Partners — operates in 78 countries with 700,000+ provider partnerships, ensuring help is always nearby wherever you travel.

Bajaj Allianz Travel Claim Process

1

Emergency Call

Contact Bajaj Allianz 24/7 travel helpline or Allianz Partners local number (listed in policy document). They coordinate hospital admission and activate cashless facility.

2

Receive Care

Visit recommended hospital for cashless treatment. For non-partner hospitals, pay upfront and collect all original documents — hospital bills, reports, prescriptions, and receipts.

3

Document Collection

Collect all evidence: medical records, bills, police FIR (theft/loss), airline communication (delays), boarding passes, baggage receipts, and reference numbers from assistance provider.

4

File & Settle

Submit claim via Caringly Yours app or website within 30 days of return. Upload documents digitally. Claims processed within 14-21 working days. NEFT settlement to bank account.

Frequently Asked Questions — Bajaj Allianz Travel

Is Bajaj Allianz travel insurance accepted for Schengen visa?

Yes, Bajaj Allianz has a dedicated Schengen Travel Insurance plan that meets all visa requirements — €30,000+ medical cover, repatriation of remains, and emergency evacuation. The policy certificate explicitly mentions Schengen compliance and is accepted by all 27 Schengen country embassies.

What is the advantage of Allianz's global network?

Allianz SE is the world's #1 insurer with 700,000+ medical providers across 78 countries. This means Bajaj Allianz policyholders have access to the largest assistance network globally — faster hospital coordination, wider cashless coverage, and more reliable emergency support than any other Indian travel insurer.

How much does Bajaj Allianz travel insurance cost for Europe?

A 10-day Europe trip (Schengen) with Travel Guard Gold ($150K cover) costs approximately ₹2,000-4,000 depending on age. Platinum variant ($500K) costs ₹4,000-7,000. Student annual plans for Europe range from ₹10,000-22,000.

Does Bajaj Allianz cover COVID-19 medical expenses abroad?

Yes, Bajaj Allianz travel plans cover COVID-19 related medical emergencies (hospitalization, treatment) abroad as part of the standard medical cover. Trip cancellation due to COVID diagnosis is also covered in eligible plans subject to terms and conditions.

Can students get mental health coverage in travel insurance?

Yes, Bajaj Allianz Student Travel Secure plan includes mental health counseling coverage abroad — covering consultation fees for anxiety, depression, and adjustment disorders that students commonly face when studying overseas. A unique and progressive benefit.
